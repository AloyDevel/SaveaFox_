What is Save a Fox

We rescue foxes from fur farms and elsewhere.

The purpose of this website is not for profit of any kind. It is to educate people on the suffering of an animal that is perpetuated by the fur trade. Join with in helping save these beautiful animals. I do not breed my foxes. I only rescue them to take care of any health issues, spay, or neuter and find them good homes.

Our rescue process

Rescue and care of baby foxes

Preparation and care of fox cubs

We prepare for fox cubs by having all the necessary supplies such as medicine, feeding syringes, teats and blankets. When we rescue foxes older than 7 weeks, we need ample space and regular veterinary appointments to ensure the safety and well-being of our puppies.

We rescue foxes from anywhere

Rescuing and helping

We rescue foxes from many different situations. We rescue sick, injured, and orphaned foxes from furfarms. Ones that the farmers believe would not make it. We take in surrendered foxes that were once pets and originally came from breeders. We also take in and rescue found pet foxes.

Give love and domesticate

Love and affection

The foxes we rescue need love to thrive. Without love, they grow wild and unmanageable, they are animals that need a lot of attention to be given love and play, not everyone is suited to care for a fox, if you are interested you can learn more with our guide on "How to care for a fox".

Adopt and find a home

Our goal is to give all the foxes we rescue up for adoption. Some foxes I rescue tend to be more feral by nature; Those are the foxes I would choose not to adopt. But we do our best to make them human-friendly and safe.

How you can help?

I'm very thankful for each and every person that has given to SaveAFox cause. Below are the three ways you can help rescue foxes from fur farms.

Donate

You can make a donation to show your love for the foxes and our work by helping to fund the sanctuary so that we can continue the care and needs of the foxes.

Sponsor

You can show us your support and love for foxes by contributing to improve our facilities and making our work easier and more efficient to save and shelter more foxes.

Adopt

Adopting is one of the best ways to take care of a fox, you can adopt the one you want as long as you are able and have the ideal space for the little ball of fur to live healthy and comfortably.

Fight to save a fox

Foxes are suffering at fur farms. But you can help! I encourage you to take a look at the pictures of what it is like for a fox at the fur farm. By making a donation you can help the already rescued fur farm foxes thrive. Without donations, I cannot keep up with all the food, medical expenses, and housing for these foxes. Or YOU can rescue your own fur farm fox by adopting or sponsoring.

Although fur farms are not illegal in the USA you can still help bring an end to the fur industry simply by not buying fur. Eighty-five percent of fur available for purchase comes from the animals living in the fur industry with the fox being one of the most commonly used.
